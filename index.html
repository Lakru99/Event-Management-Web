About Us

Planning memorable events that delight guests effortlessly is an exceptionally difficult feat. That’s why you should turn to experienced event
organisers for private and corporate event planning. Based in Derby, 4Events skilfully produces well-organised events, including corporate and
weddings, games, entertainment, and simulators. The result of our tireless work is a tremendous occasion that leaves guests truly exhilarated.

Bolstered by vast organisational excellence and comprehensive health and safety standards, our company also possesses robust Public Liability
insurance. Courteous, professional, and efficient, 4Events should be your first port of call when you need unmatched event planning.
